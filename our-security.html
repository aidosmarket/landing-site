A Unique Approach, A Unique Team

Aidos Market is unique in our approach to securing your crypto currencies. A few cornerstones of our strategy are the following:

Our Security Team

Our diverse team includes people with foundations in the world of technology, banking, commodity and currency professionals, as well as experts in data security and intelligence. We are constantly improving our platform by analyzing any potential issue from all possible angles to ensure maximum security.

Ultra-Secure Bitcoin Storage

We take strenuous measures to secure Bitcoin storage above and beyond the industry standard. This includes but is not limited to secure cold storage with multi-sig environment.

Constant Monitoring

Programmatic security is never sufficient without human judgment. For this reason, our technical team maintains constant vigilance, monitoring all movements for any suspicious activity.

Securing Your Account

By default, new accounts are set up to use email verification for all sensitive operations, such as withdrawals and settings changes. Even so, we recommend configuring your cellphone to provide two-factor authentication (2FA) for your account. Two-factor authentication (2FA) can be set up by visiting the security page on your account and following the instructions shown. It will provide an extra layer of security for your account.
