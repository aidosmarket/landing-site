How Does Aidos Market Work

1. Registration

demoTo start trading bitcoins on Aidos Market, simply go to the registration page and fill in a brief registration form (5 fields). Then log in with the user name and password that you received in your mail and you are set!

2. Bitcoin Funding

You can fund your account with Bitcoin by visiting the deposit page and following the instructions. You can also manage and obtain alternative Bitcoin addresses for your account by visiting the bitcoin addresses page. You may only obtain one new address every 1 hour.

3. Order Entry

At the moment there in one type of an order: LIMIT. This order can be used to enter or exit the market. The order entry ticket is simple yet versatile and will offer up a confirmation screen before it goes to the market. Open orders can then be easily edited or canceled on the open orders page.
